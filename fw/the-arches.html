The Arches
Fineshade Wood
Nr Corby
Northants
NN17 3BB

Map

10am-4pm daily

In 2018, Fermynwoods Contemporary Art expanded their programme to The Arches, a space renovated by the Forestry Commission in Fineshade Wood, a natural habitat and ancient mixed broadleaf and conifer woodland, heritage site and leisure facility in Northamptonshire, 13 miles outside of Corby. The Arches is a project space and artist studio for The Forest Is the Museum, our programme of artists in residence and associated activity, as well as a venue for public events.
